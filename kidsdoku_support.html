How do I play Kidsdoku?
Kidsdoku works just like Sudoku but with icons instead of numbers! Each row, column, and box must contain each icon exactly once. Tap an empty cell and choose the correct icon to fill it in.
What age is Kidsdoku suitable for?
Kidsdoku is designed for children ages 4 and up. The 3x3 grid is perfect for beginners, while older children can enjoy the challenge of 4x4 and 6x6 grids.
How do in-app purchases work?
Kidsdoku offers optional premium features through in-app purchases, including additional icon themes and advanced difficulty levels. All purchases are processed securely through the App Store.
Is an internet connection required?
No! Kidsdoku works completely offline. Once downloaded, your child can play anytime, anywhere without needing an internet connection.
How do I restore my purchases?
Open the app settings and tap "Restore Purchases". Your previous purchases will be automatically restored to your device.
Is my child's data safe?
Absolutely! We take privacy seriously. Kidsdoku does not collect any personal information from children. For more details, please see our Privacy Policy.
