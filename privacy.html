Welcome to Kidsdoku! This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our fun sudoku puzzle app designed for kids. We are committed to protecting the privacy of all our users, especially children.

üì±Information We Collect

Kidsdoku is designed to be a safe puzzle game with minimal data collection. Here's what you need to know:

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (names, emails, addresses)
  • Photos or media files
  • Precise location data
  • Contact information
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes

Local Data Storage: The app may store the following information locally on your device only:

  • Game progress and puzzle completion status
  • User preferences (sound settings, theme choices)
  • High scores and achievements

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

üìäAnalytics Data

To improve Kidsdoku and understand how the app is used, we collect anonymous, aggregated analytics data through Firebase Analytics. This information is collected automatically and does not personally identify users.

Analytics data we collect includes:

  • App usage statistics (which features are used, session duration)
  • Device information (device model, operating system version)
  • General location (country or region only, not precise location)
  • App performance data (crashes, errors)
  • In-app events (puzzle completions, level progress)

Important: This analytics data is anonymized and aggregated. It cannot be used to identify individual users or children.
